Arabic Coffee

Arabic coffee is the name of coffee which is used in Arab countries and Middle East. Arabic has been severed in a special pot called Dallah and the coffee cups are small with no handles, the portions are very small, covering the bottom of the cup. It is served in houses and in good restaurants by specially clad waiters called gahwaji, and it is almost always accompanied with dates.

Arabic coffee with dates is the tradition of Arabs, and they offered it most social events. We also welcome you in our Dubai desert safari camp with traditional Arabic coffee with dates; they are served just when you enter in the Dubai desert safari camp and it’s free while you are in our desert safari camp.
